Problem of the Year

Every year, the HCMMC team awards one problem on the contest with POTY, Problem of the Year, as a way of recognizing expertise in writing beautiful problems with ingenuitive and inventive solutions. We commemorate these problem writers for their splendid contribution not only to our contest but also to the art of problem writing.
